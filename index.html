I've been building websites for a few years now. Started the way most people do — curious, a bit lost, figuring things out by breaking them. Now I build things that actually hold up, look right, and do what they're supposed to do.

I'm self-taught and still learning (Laravel and C++ are current rabbit holes). I think staying a learner is actually what makes the work better — I don't assume I know everything, so I actually look for the right solution instead of just the familiar one.

Hire me and you get someone who gives a damn about the thing working — not just technically, but for whatever you're trying to pull off. I'll be straight with you about timelines, straight about what I can't do, and I'll stay on it until it's right.
